## Question:
Who can use MVP?
## Answer:
Businesses, individuals, and representatives such as title clerks, agents, and dealerships can all use the Motor Vehicle Portal (MVP). Specifically:
- **Businesses** can manage e-lien, fleet, and Motor Vehicle Enforcement Division (MVED) accounts.
- **Individuals** can perform many Division of Motor Vehicle (DMV) functions like requesting personalized license plates.
- **Agents** can manage clients’ Utah vehicle accounts [doc1].
