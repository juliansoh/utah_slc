## Question: 
How much is my Impound?
## Answer: 
The fees associated with impoundment in Utah can vary based on the reason for the impound. Below is a summary of the common impound release fees:

- **Improper/Expired/No Registration impound release**: Equal to the registration fee, plus any additional fees required at the time of release [doc1].
- **DUI (Driving Under the Influence) impound release**: $455.00 [doc1].
- **BUI (Boating Under the Influence) impound release**: $455.00 [doc1].
- **Ignition Interlock System (IIS) violation impound release**: $455.00 [doc1].
- **Exhibition Driving impound release**: $455.00 [doc1].
- **Driver’s license revoked due to drug or alcohol-related issues impound release**: $455.00 [doc1].
- **Revoked No Insurance impound release**: $100.00 [doc1].
- **Road rage impound release**: $455.00 [doc1].

Additionally, if the vehicle is not currently registered, all registration fees will also be required before the vehicle can be released [doc4]. Towing and storage fees may also be assessed by the impound yard where the vehicle is held [doc1].
