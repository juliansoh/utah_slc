## Question:
"My registration was revoked for no insurance. How do I reinstate it, and is there a fine?"

## Answer:
To reinstate your vehicle registration after it has been revoked for no insurance, you need to follow these steps:

1. **Visit the DMV Office:** Go to your local DMV office at the time of your appointment.
2. **Present Evidence of Ownership:** Provide documentation such as the vehicle’s title or registration card.
3. **Present Picture Identification:** Show a valid photo ID.
4. **Provide Proof of Insurance:** Present one of the following as evidence of insurance coverage for the vehicle:
   - A current form SR-22 obtained from a Utah licensed insurance company or agent
   - A current insurance policy
   - A current insurance binder
   - A current insurance declaration
   - A current insurance card
5. **Pay the Reinstatement Fee:** A $100 reinstatement fee will be assessed unless you can provide proof that the vehicle was insured on the date that the registration was revoked [doc1][doc3].
