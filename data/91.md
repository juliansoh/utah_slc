## Question:
Where do I get a 2290?

## Answer:
You can obtain IRS Form 2290, "Schedule of Heavy Highway Vehicles," either online or in person at a local IRS office. Here are the details:

1. **Online**: Copies of the form 2290 may be obtained online from the [Utah DMV website](aHR0cHM6Ly9zdGFkdmVudHVyZXdvcmtzZGV2MDAxLmJsb2IuY29yZS53aW5kb3dzLm5ldC91dGFoZG12L2h0bWwvZm9ybTIyOTAuaHRtbA2) or directly from the IRS website.

2. **In Person**:
   - **Salt Lake Office**: 178 South Rio Grande Street, Salt Lake City, UT 84101
   - **Ogden Office**: 324 25th Street, Ogden, UT 84401

   Before visiting an office to pay or to file your form, you must make an appointment by calling 844-545-5640. The office hours for both locations are 8:30 am – 4:30 PM [doc1].
