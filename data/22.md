## Question:
Why isn’t my vintage vehicle registration only $10.00?

## Answer:
Your vintage vehicle registration may not be only $10.00 if your vehicle is a model year 1983 or newer. According to Utah law, vintage vehicles with model years 1983 and newer are required to pay vintage registration fees as per Utah Code 41-1a-1206 [doc1].
