## Question:
Can the dealer drive the consigned vehicle on the consignor’s license plates if they are still current?

## Answer:
No, the dealer cannot drive the consigned vehicle on the consignor's license plates, even if they are still current. The dealer must remove the consignor's license plates and/or any temporary permits on the vehicle. The vehicle can only be operated using dealer plates [doc1].
